## Album Cover Layout Rules

- Centered minimal subject → large negative space (70–80% of frame)
- Typography is a ghost, not a headline (barely readable, smeared, faded stamp marks)
- Texture field dominates: subject is implied, not dominant
- Memory materials: matte sleeve texture, faded liner notes, sleeve wear, archival sticker residue
- Palette: sepia_zine or nocturne_neon or cd_iridescent
